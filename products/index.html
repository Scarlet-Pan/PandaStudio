PolicyPal （保管）

AI policy companion · not a licensed broker; we do not sell coverage or file claims for you

App ID / Bundle ID: studio.panda.policy

You’re not alone with your policies.

A local-first app to import policy PDFs, keep structured records on your device, and ask a bring-your-own-key (BYOK) model about the policies you link — with costs you control.

What it helps you do

Easier day to day

  • Import on-device policy PDFs into a local archive
  • See coverage highlights at a glance
  • Ask about hospitals, conditions, and care paths
  • Keep multi-turn chat tied to the same policy
  • Avoid flipping dozens of PDF pages in a hurry

Safer by design

  • Policies and chats stay on your phone
  • No account and no developer backend that collects your data
  • BYOK — you bring the model API key and pay the provider
  • AI answers are for reference; insurer wording prevails
  • Key stored with platform secure storage where implemented

Tool software · data stays on-device · AI for reference only

PolicyPal is AI-assisted tool software. Hangzhou Panda Studio Technology Co., Ltd. is not an insurer, insurance broker, insurance agent, or claims adjuster. The app does not sell insurance or file claims on your behalf.

保管 （PolicyPal）

AI 保单伙伴 · 非持牌经纪、非代投代赔

App ID / Bundle ID: studio.panda.policy

你不是一个人在管你的保单

本地优先：导入本机保单 PDF、在设备上建立档案，并用自带密钥（BYOK）的大模型围绕已关联保单问答——费用由你自行控制。

能帮用户做什么

用起来省心

  • 导入本机 PDF，建立本地保单档案
  • 列表摘要一眼看清保障重点
  • 按医院、病情、就医路径追问
  • 多轮对话始终围绕同一份保单
  • 紧急时刻少翻几十页条款

用起来安心

  • 保单与聊天留在本机
  • 无账号、无自研后端采集用户数据
  • BYOK：自带模型 Key，费用自控
  • AI 解读仅供参考，以保司核赔为准
  • API Key 使用系统级安全存储（已实现处）
